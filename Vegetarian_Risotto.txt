Vegetarian Risotto Recipe

1. Melt the butter with oil in a big pot and fry the onions, beetroots and carrots for 10 minutes. 
2. Add the rice, cook and mix for 2 more minutes.
3. Pour the red wine and boil untill it evaporates. 
4. Add ladle of stock everytime the rice absorbes it. This process should take around 25 minutes.
3. Add salt and peppers and serve instantly with sour cream and chives spread on top.
