Bruschetta Recipe

Toppings Preperation
-slice olives, tomatoes and onions into small cubes
-add salt, pepper and oil according to your liking
-mix them

Bread Preperation
-toast the bread
-spread a thin layer of garlic butter over one side of the bread
-using a spoon add some of the toppings on the bread
-place in a pre-heated oven for a few minutes untill the bread is crunchy
-plate the bruschetta and serve while still warm
